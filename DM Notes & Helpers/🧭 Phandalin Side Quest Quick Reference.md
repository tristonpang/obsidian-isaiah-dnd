
## 🪓 1. Old Owl Well (Orcs & Undead)

- **Given by:** **Daran Edermath**
- **📍 Location:** _Edermath Orchard (south of Phandalin)_
- **Faction:** Order of the Gauntlet
- **Hook:** Orc sightings + undead activity near Old Owl Well

**NPC Personality**

- Calm, honorable, retired adventurer
- Values justice, discipline, and duty
- Encourages bravery and righteousness

**Details**

- Investigate ruins → encounter **Hamun Kost (Red Wizard)** + zombies
- Can become diplomatic or hostile

**Faction Angle**

- Tests PCs for Order of the Gauntlet recruitment
